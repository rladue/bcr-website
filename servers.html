BCR ORIGINAL PVPVE

Our very first server, Original PvPvE offers players the ability to choose when to engage in PvP combat.

The server features PvP zones at all major monuments, and temporary PvP zones at Patrol Helicopter and Bradley crash sites. Players' bases cannot be raided, unless the owners have been offline for over four-consecutive days.

Stealing is allowed, but griefing, harrassment and overly toxic behavior is not tolerated.

Features:

  • Purge before wipe, PvP and raiding is enabled for the final 36 hours of each wipe
  • Custom 4x loot table and 4x gather rate
  • Custom Raidable NPC Bases with NPC defenders
  • Abandoned Bases after 96 hours (4 days) offline
  • Additional Bradley that drives along the ring road across the entire map
  • Spawn personal vehicles
  • Backpacks
  • Enhanced Hammer
  • Remover tool
  • Zombie Hordes
  • Dangerous Treasures
  • Quicker crafting, smelting and recycling
  • Teleportation
  • Clans with auto authorization
  • Chest stacking
  • Player Ranks
  • Quick Sort
  • Furnace Splitter

Plugins unique to BCR:

  • Shoot-to-Detonate Explosives
  • Repairbench takes resources directly from TC
  • Composter Splitter

BCR LITE PVE

Lite PvE is aimed toward players seeking a more casual pace of play without the constant threat of being killed by other players.

Players are not allowed to kill, or steal from, players that are not in their clan. Players' bases cannot be raided, unless the owners have been offline for over four-consecutive days.

The server features PvE raidable bases, zombie hordes, and additional Bradley's at major monuments, including one that drives around the ring road.

Features:

BCR BRICKYARD PVP

Our newest server, Brickyard is a solo/duo/trio full-PvP server that has relaxed community rules on toxic behavior, allowing for a more iconic Rust style of play. Rules regarding racism, hate speech and threats are still enforced.

Like the other servers, Brickyard PvP features the same 4x gather rate, and custom loot table. Additionally, the majority of QoL plugins are also featured, including personal vehicles and teleportation.

There are Raidable Bases that spawn on the map, and player bases can be raided at any time.

Features:

  • Custom 4x loot table and 4x gather rate
  • Custom Raidable NPC Bases with NPC defenders
  • Additional Bradley that drives along the ring road across the entire map
  • Spawn personal vehicles
  • Backpacks
  • Enhanced Hammer
  • Remover tool
  • Zombie Hordes
  • Quicker crafting, smelting and recycling
  • Teleportation w/ Combat and Raid Block enabled
  • Clans with auto authorization
  • Chest stacking
  • Player Ranks
  • Quick Sort
  • Furnace Splitter

Plugins unique to BCR:

  • Shoot-to-Detonate Explosives
  • Repairbench takes resources directly from TC
  • Composter Splitter
